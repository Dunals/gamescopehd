Description : "Seven castles" is a real-time, multiplayer board game in which you build castles, roads or cloisters by placing tiles on the board. the one who pays for it - leaves before the end f the game no credits are lost.\n\nHow to play\n\nThis is a turn based multiplayer board game. \n\nWhen your turn arrives you will see your tile in the bottom right corner.\n\nPlace your tile by tapping on the board so that it matches the existing ones.\n\nYou can zoom the game are by pinch or with the zoom buttons.\n\nYou can rotate with the rotate button on the right side. The tile will only rotate into good positions.\n\nPut one of your meeples (the little avatars at the top) on the tile by clicking on a red circle.
