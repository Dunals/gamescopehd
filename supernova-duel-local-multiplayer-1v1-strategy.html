The hard part was to make a simple strategy game that is cut down to the most core strategic elements. It was exciting to try and convert the feeling from strategy board games into a real time experience. Unfortunately, it is not practical to have real time strategy games without digital assistance, so the iPad was chosen as optimal platform for this experiment. \nI wanted the game to feature recognizable elements from normal strategy games: \n- Upgrading (tap and hold star)\n- Attack (send out comets towards enemy star)\n- Defense (send out comets against incoming enemy comets)\n- Resource management (balancing attack, defense and upgrading)\n- Tactics (choose an unpredictable route for your comets or make a timing attack when opponent is weak after upgrading)\n\nGood luck - have fun!\nPlease support us by leaving a review, if you like the game :)"
