Description : "Fire Block Battle is a new type of puzzle game which is played by two persons (or with computer).\nThe target of the game is to burn out all of the opponent blocks.\nTo do that, we delete blocks in a chain, send fire ball to the opponent, and disturb the move of the opponent.\nFire Block Battle is a game which 8x8 block is pre-arranged and the blocks can be move freely. Due to the freely movable characteristic, it is easier to construct long chain in Fire Block Battle even for a beginner.\nWe can battle not only with computer, but also with a person and by online so that we can always find a new opponent with a new stategy, which will avoid us to get bored.\n\nHow to play:\nIn Fire Block Battle, we have to connect 4 or more fire blocks to delete them. The deleted fire blocks become fire balls which are send to your opponent and burn out his/her blocks.\n\nYou can move the fire block freely from anywhere to anywhere, but not the burnt blocks, called kurosuke. When your field is occupied by a lot of kurosuke and has no more move, you will lose.\n\nThe blocks over the deleted blocks will fall down and may create a sequence of chains of deleting blocks. The longer the chain, the more fire balls will be sent to your opponent.\n\nSix dojos with eight masters/apprentices will help you sharpening your skill. Learn how they arrange the blocks to create a long chain. Beat them and get their treasures to show your skill.\n\nEvery player has a Power, which is a measure of how strong is him/her. Every time you win, your power will increase, vice versa. The stronger opponent you beat, the more power increase will you gain.\n\nIf you feel your skill has improved much and want to prove it, just go to Battle Field where you can find other players over the world.\n\nTips for a beginner:\n- Play at Forest Dojo from Apprentice 6 to Apprentice 1 to get accustomed with the game. It is easy to beat Apprentice 6, but hard to beat Apprentice 1. You have to find moves which can make 2 or more chains. Luck might be an important factor at this stage. Don't quit the game at this stage, the true sense of the game is not the luck, but the ability to create a long chain which can be learned at the next dojos. If you get stuck in beating apprentices in Forest Dojo, let's go to Practice mode and take a peek at dojo training. You should get some hint there.\n- After beat Apprentice 1 of Forest Dojo, go to Sea Dojo. Try battle with the apprentices and see how they arrange the blocks. As you learned how to create chains, a new world of Fire Block Battle will opened in the front of you."
